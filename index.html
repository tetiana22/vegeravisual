About me

I’m a Nottinghamshire-based freelance photographer specialising in live events, concerts, and performances. What inspires me most is the raw emotion, light, and movement that make every show unique. Through my lens, I aim to capture the energy and authenticity of each moment — preserving not just the image, but the feeling behind it. You can find most of my recent work on Instagram and Facebook, and selected collections are showcased here on my portfolio page. For bookings or collaborations, feel free to reach out via email or social media.
